Financial Insights

Understanding the industry from a student's perspective

My current focus is on understanding the specialised regulations and accounting principles in industries such as the natural resources, agriculture, energy, mining, automotives, and manufacturing. These industries require an in-depth understanding of how businesses must comply with strict environmental and operational laws, and I want to be well-equipped in contributing to the compliance and sustainable growth of such businesses.

Alongside this, I am planning on taking my RE5 (which allows me to provide financial advice in South Africa) and NCA exams (which focus on laws governing consumer credit). These will ensure that I am versatile and prepared for a diverse role in financial service and retail industries—industries that offer fast-paced and dynamic challenges which I would like to explore.

It feels like being at a crossroad with many amazing paths to choose from, and I’m giving myself the room to learn and discover which industry resonates with me the most. And this section, beloved reader, is where I’ll share my opinions and all that I learn along the way—in theory and practically.
